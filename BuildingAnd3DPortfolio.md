**🧱 3D & Building Portfolio**

Below is an overview of my 3D building work, environment design, and structural modeling on Roblox.
I began my journey in 2016 as a builder, long before I touched scripting. Over the years, I shifted toward programming and systems engineering — but I never abandoned building. Instead, I learned to combine both disciplines.

Most of the structures and assets shown here were created out of necessity: I often needed environments, props, or accessories that simply didn’t exist or didn’t meet the quality I wanted. This pushed me to develop my own 3D workflow using only Roblox tools, focusing on clean geometry, modular design, and performance‑friendly assets.

Today, building remains a core part of my identity as a developer.
It complements my scripting background and allows me to create complete, functional, and visually coherent experiences. :)
